01_WHAT_THIS_IS

The Ship Loop is not another course.

It's a weekly rhythm with 20 other builders and me, aimed at one outcome: a deployed product with paying users by day 30.

You bring your own idea, or you use the cohort's first week to find one. From there, every week has a job: validate, blueprint, ship, sell. You show up to the calls. You post progress. You get unstuck in the channel. If you do the work, you ship.

You don't graduate by watching a video. You graduate by shipping.
02_THE_WEEK

Three live things per week.

Mon
90 min · live

Ship Loop Call

The week's stage — Ideate, Validate, Blueprint, Ship, or Sell. We walk it together. Members present progress, get feedback, unstick in public. This is the backbone of the cohort.

Wed
90 min · live · optional

Build Session

Co-working call. Screen-share if you want feedback, work silent if you don't. I'm in the room. Use the hour to actually build, not to research.

Fri
async · in channel

Ship Board Review

Post the week's shipped work. I review and give feedback on one or two per week. The Board is where the social proof lives — it's how you watch other members' work and know you're not alone at this.

06_FAQ

Questions you probably have

Why is the cohort capped at 20?
Because the room breaks past that. 20 is the number where feedback stays fast, the channel stays readable, and I can actually see each person's progress on the Ship Board. If seats run out before you join, the next cohort opens within a few weeks — join Build Room at $9/mo to hold your spot and get the notification first.
What if I don't ship?
I'll coach you through the next cohort free if you followed the process and didn't ship. If you didn't follow the process — didn't show up to calls, didn't post in the channel — that's on you. The format is the accountability; it only works if you use it.
Can I pause if I have a work emergency?
Don't join if you're about to have a work emergency. For real ones that happen mid-cohort, email me — I'll move you to the next cohort. Don't make a habit of it.
Is $297/mo cohort-only, or does it continue?
Month-to-month. Most people do 1–2 months — one to ship, one optional to iterate. After that, the alumni channel is free. You're not locked in. You're in until you ship, then you leave.
What if I want 1:1 instead of group?
There's a 1:1 option (Ship & Sell) for people who want me personally walking them through the 30 days. It's waitlist-only and I take a handful of clients at a time. Join the cohort first and mention it in the channel — if it's the right fit, I'll send you the memo.
